# The VC Brain

**AI-first venture sourcing & diligence — surface exceptional founders and produce an evidence-backed investment decision in 24 hours.**

Built for the [Hack-Nation Global AI Hackathon #6](https://hack-nation.ai) — *The VC Brain* challenge (Maschmeyer Group × Hack-Nation).

## The problem
Capital flows through networks, not merit. Founders stay invisible until they know the right person; their story is scattered across pitch decks, GitHub repos, half-built sites and social posts. Great ideas die not for lack of merit, but for lack of a fair shot at being seen.

## What it does
An AI operating system for the front of the venture funnel — **Sourcing → Screening → Diligence → Decision**:

- **Sourcing** — surfaces strong founders before they fundraise (inbound applications + outbound signal scanning: GitHub, launches, hackathons, papers, ProductHunt).
- **Founder Score** — a persistent, evidence-backed profile that sharpens over time, with a deliberate focus on the **cold-start / no-track-record** founder.
- **Screening** — three independent axes (Founder / Market / Idea-vs-Market), scored separately, never averaged.
- **Diligence & Trust Score** — every claim traced to a source with a calibrated confidence level; external verification where possible, contradictions flagged.
- **Decision** — a transparent, citation-backed investment memo.

## Stack (planned)
LLM reasoning & extraction, embeddings for founder-signal matching, web/data ingestion, and a lightweight investor-facing UX.
